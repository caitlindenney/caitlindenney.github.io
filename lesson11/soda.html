Soda Springs Idaho.

Soda Springs Idaho has a population of 2,985 people. The year it was founded was 1858. Their town motto is “Historic Oregon Trail Oasis. The Soda is on Us”. It is the home of the soda spring geyser. The geyser was first made in 1934 when the “town fathers” were looking for hot water to make a bathing place. They drilled into a chamber of highly pressurized carbon dioxide gas and cold water and the geyser was released. It flooded the downtown area for weeks until it was capped. It is now let loose every hour for a tourist attraction.
