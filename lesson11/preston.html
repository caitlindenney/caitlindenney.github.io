Preston Idaho.

Preston Idaho has a population of 5,204 people. The year it was founded was 1866. Their town motto is “Home of Napoleon Dynamite” (much of the movie Napoleon Dynamite was shot there in 2004). The town was first found by pioneers from the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. The town name was first called Worm Creek.
